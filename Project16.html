Covid Resources App

In this time of pandemic we all are trying aur best to keep united and fight Corona virus and keeping this in our mind me and my friend Shantanu soam has developed a non-profit android app that fetches the tweets of your city regarding the availability and requirements of the covid resources such as oxygen, remdesivir, ICU beds, plasma etc. so that those who are in need of them or who wants to help someone can directly collect every data in a single app. I would request you all to just share this app to others as much as you can as a fight against covid so that this app could reach to someone in need!

Let's obey our duty to be united and fight against this virus by supporting each other.

Some instructions to install this app if any problems occurs!

- Follow the link shared on Google Chrome or opera instead of using default browser.

- Allow all the permissions required to install external app.

- Clean your device memory if apk unables to install.
